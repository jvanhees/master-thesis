Measure 3 things with the survey:

- Engagement
Does the (video)thumbnail improve the willingness to click on the article summary?

- Appeal
Does the (video)thumbnail improve the overal look and feel of the article summary?

- Informativeness
Does the user feel better informed over the article?



A/B test with 3 possible scenarios:
- Title + description + video placeholder (?)
- Title + description + thumbnail
- Title + description + videothumbnail

After introduction, an article title and description is shown to the user, along with a placeholder image. After going to the next step, the following three statements are made, along with a Likert scale input:

- I am interested in viewing the stated video.
